For this particular project, we were mostly interested in the following questions:

  1. Did the number of pitchers used in a game increase over time (both between seasons and within seasons)?
    • Dashboard
  2. Do different umpires, ballparks, or teams have more runs, home runs, strike-outs, or walks than others in the 21st century?
    • Tables for Umpires
    • Ballpark Map
  3. What day of the week is there the highest batting average? Is this different between weekdays and weekends? What about across months?
    • Tables
  4. How has the game evolved between regular season play and the postseason? These questions will consist of time of game, home and away team game statistics, and home-field advantage or win probability, based on whether the games are regular season or postseason.
    • Dashboard (Statistics)
    • Dashboard (Hit types)
    • Tables
